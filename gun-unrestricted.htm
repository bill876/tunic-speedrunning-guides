Get Stick

Acquire the stick and equip the bomb

  • Enter the small house with a mailbox.
  • Immediately roll toward and open the chest.
  • While opening the chest, equip the Firecracker.
  • As soon as the Stick dialog ends, pause, quit, and continue.
  • Immediately exit the house.

Use Bomb

Run west of Overworld Save and throw bomb

  • Run up the stairs, being sure to draw the cleanest line.
  • Move left of the pillars approaching the save point.
  • Cross the bridge and approach the key.
  • Throw the bomb at the bushes.
  • While waiting for the bomb, equip the Stick.

LS to Quarry

Use Ladder Storage to fly over to Quarry

  • Activate Ladder Storage on the now-accessible ladder.
  • Run directly left around the closed gate and toward Quarry.
  • Run past the fuse and into the Quarry.
  • Run past the second fuse and to the save point.

Get Sniped

Use Ladder Storage until a sniper downs you

  • Activate Ladder Storage on the ladder behind the save.
  • Run directly left and curve around just beside the sniper.
  • Continue up-left, past the sniper and towards a chest.
  • The sniper should fire and down you just beside another scavenger.
  • Run to the left and up to the passage to the shooting range.
