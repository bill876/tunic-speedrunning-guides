Start

Use the "Plushie Code" to start early and get bomb

  • As soon as the in-game timer starts, type in the Plushie code.
  • While the fox shows the plushie, obtain the free firecracker.
  • Leave while the camera is still zooming.

Get Stick

Acquire the stick and equip the bomb

  • Enter the small house with a mailbox.
  • Immediately roll toward and open the chest.
  • While opening the chest, equip the Firecracker.
  • As soon as the Stick dialog ends, pause, quit, and continue.
  • Immediately exit the house.

Use Bomb

Run west of Overworld Save and throw bomb

  • Run up the stairs, being sure to draw the cleanest line.
  • Move left of the pillars approaching the save point.
  • Cross the bridge and approach the key.
  • Throw the bomb at the bushes.
  • While waiting for the bomb, equip the Stick.

LS to Quarry

Use Ladder Storage to fly over to Quarry

  • Activate Ladder Storage on the now-accessible ladder.
  • Run directly left around the closed gate and toward Quarry.
  • Run past the fuse and into the Quarry.
  • Run past the second fuse and to the save point.

Get Sniped

Use Ladder Storage until a sniper downs you

  • Activate Ladder Storage on the ladder behind the save.
  • Run directly left and curve around just beside the sniper.
  • Continue up-left, past the sniper and towards a chest.
  • The sniper should fire and down you just beside another scavenger.
  • Run to the left and up to the passage to the shooting range.

Acquire Gun

Run around the snipers and open the Gun chest

  • As you approach the shooting range, the snipers will aim at you.
  • Run in a hemispheric curve around them.
  • They should each aim, fire and miss.
  • Quickly run to the chest and open it.
  • Time stops on the frame the world freezes to display the gun.

Ladder Storage (LS)
Unrestricted

Allows you to ignore any boundaries and gravity.

How To LS

  1. Face a ladder so that it is interactable.
  2. Press your interact key and your stick/sword at the same time.
  3. Immediately mash roll (direction + A) while the attack animation is playing.
  4. If done correctly, you'll roll away and be able to walk off edges.

Important to Know

  • When you transition to a new scene, you MUST pause, quit, and continue.
  • If you let go of run at any time, you will snap back to the ladder, UNLESS
  • You can maintain LS by constantly mashing attack.
  • If you are damaged during LS, you will fall out of it.
NOTE: It may be easier to bind your interact and item keys to a single input. This is allowed and is usually done in Steam's controller settings.
