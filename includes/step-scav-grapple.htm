Boss: Scavenger (Grapple Off)

Push the Scavenger off the left side of the arena

  • Make sure the speedrunner code is active.
  • Equipment required: Orb, Dagger, Wand
  • Run up and ice grapple the Scav at max orb distance.
  • Position yourself to the right of the Scav and blast it with wand shots until the ice breaks.
  • Immediately re-freeze and grapple repeatedly until the Scav is off the left arena edge.
  • If done quickly and correctly, the Scav will pop into the death plane.
  • Obtain the Blue Key, equip a bomb, and use it to death warp.
  • Be sure to use the death animation to menu.
