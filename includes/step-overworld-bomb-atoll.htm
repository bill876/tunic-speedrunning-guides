Sneak to Atoll

Bomb through the wall and sneak into the teleporter

  1. Descend south to where you bombed the bushes and enter.
  2. Walk past the rudeling to bait a sword swing and run west to the hidden bomb chest.
  3. Walk digitally (keyboard or remapped D-pad) up-left and down-left into the wall, throw the fire bomb, and roll away. If done correctly, it should explode and burn the bushes on the other side of the wall.
  4. Run around to and through the burning bushes (to avoid aggro) and to the teleporter. Warp to Far Shore and to Atoll (lower-left).
