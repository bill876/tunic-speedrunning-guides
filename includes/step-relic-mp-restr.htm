MP Relic (Library)

Obtain the fifth relic.

  1. Warp to the Far Shore and to the Library (bottom-right). Equip Sword, Dagger, Wand, and Orange Peril Ring in the process.
  2. Dash through the area and down the ladder, and again through the next area. Make sure the speedrunner code is active.
  3. Upon entering the room with the staircases and miasma-covered Hero's Grave, dispatch the two knights. Note: Because you do not have the lantern, you will be unable to target the enemies. The best position to ice combo them from is perpendicular to them on the staircases beside them.
  4. After both are destroyed, the Hero's Grave will be uncovered. Dash, play, and dash quickly to obtain the MP Relic. As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, quit and continue.
  5. Throw a bomb and death warp.
