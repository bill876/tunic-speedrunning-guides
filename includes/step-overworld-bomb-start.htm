Bombs Start

Acquire bombs during the opening crawl

  1. As soon as the timer starts, obtain the free firecracker.
  2. After waiting two seconds, obtain the free fire bomb.
  3. Run toward the Overworld save. After ascending two sets of stairs in the center, veer left, equip both the firecracker and fire bomb, and toss the firecracker at the bushes blocking entry to the West Overworld. Continue north to the Overworld save.
  4. Save at the Overworld save.
