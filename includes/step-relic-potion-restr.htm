POTION Relic (Quarry)

Obtain the last relic.

  • Warp to the Far Shore and to the Overworld (right). Equip Sword, Orb, Bomb, and RTSR in the process.
  • Dash past the Overworld save and to the shortcut you opened near the beginning of the run.
  • Climb the shortcut ladder here and dash/climb up to the Mountain Pass.
  • Dash through the Mountain Pass and into the Quarry. Descend to the entrance of the Monastery and enter.
  • Toss a bomb and take the damage. Dash forward until the fuse just before the gate and let your health decay into peril state (-20%).
  • Dash to the back of the miasma (touch the wall) and dash back. Engage the Voidling.
If you are in RSTR range, the fight with the Voidling is routed: grapple, two hits with Sword, repeat
  • After it is destroyed, the Hero's Grave will be uncovered. Dash, play, and dash quickly to obtain the Potion Relic.
  • As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, Quit to Menu.
  • Throw a bomb and death warp back to Overworld.
