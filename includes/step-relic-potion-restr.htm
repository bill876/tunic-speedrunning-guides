Potion Relic (Quarry)

Obtain the last relic.

  1. Warp to the Far Shore and to the Overworld (right). Equip Sword, Orb, Bomb, and RTSR in the process.
  2. Dash past the Overworld save and to the shortcut you opened near the beginning of the run. Climb the shortcut ladder here and dash/climb up to the Mountain.
  3. Dash through the Mountain and into the Quarry. Descend to the entrance of the Monastery and enter.
  4. Toss a bomb and take the damage. Dash forward until the fuse just before the gate and let your health decay into peril state (-20%).
  5. Dash to the back of the miasma (touch the wall) and dash back. Engage the Voidling. If you are in RSTR range, the fight with the Voidling is routed: grapple, two hits with Sword, repeat.
  6. After it is destroyed, the Hero's Grave will be uncovered. Dash, play, and dash quickly to obtain the Potion Relic. As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, quit and continue.
  7. Throw a bomb and death warp.
