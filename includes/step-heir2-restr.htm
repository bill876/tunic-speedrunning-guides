Boss: Heir (Fight)

Fight the Heir with ice-combo

Before Fight

  • Warp to the Far Shore. Make sure RTSR is equipped.
  • Consume a hot pepper and equip sword.
  • Warp to the Heir's arena (the fight will begin immediately).

Boss Fight

  • As soon as you hit with her sword, enter the speedrunner code.
  • Run up to the Heir and execute the ice combo.
  • Time is on last hit in Phase 2. If you die in this fight, sadly the run is over as there is no way to re-enter the Heir fight quickly.
