Boss: Heir (Fight)

Fight the Heir with ice-combo

Before Fight

  1. Warp to the Far Shore. Make sure Orange Peril Ring is equipped. Consume a hot pepper and equip sword.
  2. Warp to the Heir's arena—the fight will begin immediately.

Boss Fight

  1. As soon as you are hit, enter the speedrunner code.
  2. Run up to the Heir and execute the ice combo.
  3. Time is on last hit in Phase 2. If you die in this fight, simply retry without the pepper.
