Glass Cannon

Open a door early, get items, and go to East Forest

  • Run south until you see the mailbox, and head east (right).
  • Throw a bomb at the blocking bushes and roll through.
  • Type in the filigree door code (DRULUR) and enter the door.
  • Open all three chests and exit.
  • Navigate up the ladders and steps and into East Forest Bellower room.
  • Drop to the save point and equip Glass Cannon while falling.
  • Open the chest to obtain Stick and equip while exiting.
