Peppers and Wand

Anchor at Dark Tomb, get Hot Peppers, get Wand

  • Walk north of the save and grapple left.
  • Open the chest for an ATT Upgrade (switch to Muffling Bell).
  • Run in/out of Dark Tomb to set your Continue point.
  • Run west and open the chest between the rudelings for Peppers (2).
  • Climb the nearby ladder and grapple NE twice and obtain Wand.
  • Immediately pause, quit, and continue. Activate orb and enter Dark Tomb.
