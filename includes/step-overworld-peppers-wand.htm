Peppers and Wand

Anchor at Dark Tomb, get Hot Peppers, get Wand

  1. Run north of the checkpoint and grapple left to a tuning fork. Head up and open the chest for an ATT upgrade. During the chest-opening animation, equip Muffling Bell.
  2. Run west/left, enter Dark Tomb, and immediately exit. This is to set your Continue point (and a good place for an auto-split).
  3. Run west and open the chest between the rudelings for Peppers (2). The timing is somewhat tight, but it's pretty easy if you ignore them.
  4. Climb the nearby ladder and grapple NE twice. Obtain the Wand. Immediately pause, quit, and continue. Activate orb (to place it in your paw) and re-enter Dark Tomb.
