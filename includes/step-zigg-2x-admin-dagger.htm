Admins Skip (Dagger)

Freeze both admins and power fuse before dying

  1. Run towards the admin on the right and freeze it with the dagger. Immediately roll toward and run a bit before freezing the second. Quickly activate the fuse.
  2. Run back, wait 1-2 seconds (menu for your next equipment step), then run down and cross the bridge. Descend the stairs and ladder and head toward the Boss Scavenger.
