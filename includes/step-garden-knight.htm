Boss: Garden Knight

Stun-lock with careful combos and peril state

  • Pop your Echo of Self and run north toward the Garden Knight arena.
  • Aggro a rudeling and quickly menu: swap to Orange Peril Ring, equip orb, bomb, and wand, and ideally place your cursor over orb.
  • By this time, you should be damaged down to peril state from the rudelings.
  • Run north / NE to the ladder and enter the boss arena.

Boss Fight

  • Try to position yourself directly in line with boss and open with two full combos (1-2-3).
  • The boss will backpedal. Roll forward and do a short combo (1-2). This should break poise and stagger the boss.
  • Repeat the first step (two full combos). If the boss backpedals, roll forward. Perform a short combo (1-2).
  • One or two more hits should finish the boss.
  • Run towards the exit while the camera focuses on the boss.
  • Open the chest for 200 bits. If you have more than 1 HP, switch to Orb.
