DEF Relic (West Garden)

Obtain the second relic.

  1. Execute the wake-up dash sequence.
  2. At the end of this sequence, generally you can dash 3x North and begin dashing West. Be sure to stop to refill Stamina after 6 dashes max.
  3. Enter the furnace, exit the other side, and enter West Garden. Dash to the Hero's Grave, warp, and dash to the DEF Relic. As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, quit and continue.
  4. Throw a bomb and death warp back to Overworld.
