The Library

Pray at the powered warp and enter Library

  1. Run down to the center of Atoll, avoiding crabs. Pray at the powered device, warp, and enter the Library.
  2. Walk up the right set of stairs, up the ladder, through the next room, and up another ladder.
  3. Orb to the tuning fork and equip Sword as you travel. Ascend ladder to the librarian.
