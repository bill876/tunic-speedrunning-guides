The Library

Pray at the powered warp and enter Library

  • Run down to the center of Atoll, avoiding crabs.
  • Pray at the powered device, warp, and enter the Library.
  • Walk up the right set of stairs, up the ladder, through the next room, and up another ladder.
  • Orb to the tuning fork and equip Sword as you travel.
  • Ascend ladder to the librarian.
