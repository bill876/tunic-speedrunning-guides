To Frog's Domain

Ignore the crabs

  1. Walk left (down the stairs), cross through the middle, and to the NE (the top of screen space).
  2. Divert right at the crabs and 180 into the brick walkway.
  3. Open the chest for 15 bits.
  4. Climb the ladder, screen transition, and descend into Frog's Domain, cutting the corners the best you can.
