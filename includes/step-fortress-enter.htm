Enter Forest Fortress Area

Damage down along the way

Two options here, both with similar times:

Option 1 Faster
  1. Grapple up to the tuning fork NE of Overworld Shrine.
  2. Grapple to the next tuning for to the east.
  3. Target and ice-grapple the blue slime below.
  4. Run to the group of slimes and damage down to critical.
  5. Enter the Forest Fortress area.
Option 2 Easier
  1. Grapple up to the tuning fork NE of Overworld Shrine.
  2. Activate the shortcut lever.
  3. As soon as it pops up slightly, pause/quit/continue.
  4. Run to the group of slimes and damage down to critical.
  5. Enter the Forest Fortress area.
