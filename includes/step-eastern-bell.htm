Eastern Bell

  • The chest here is a full flask. It is never necessary but a good backup. Taking it burns about 5 seconds. It's completely up to you!
  • Climb the ladders and stairs up to the Eastern Bell. Strike the Bell with the stick. Make sure Glass Cannon is unequipped—you cannot strike the bell if it is enabled.
  • As soon as you strike the bell, walk right to the coiled rope and drop it. Equip Glass Cannon while the animation is playing.
  • Climb down the rope, head left, burn the bushes blocking the shortcut, and exit. Depending on the random location of the slimes, either avoid or kill them.
