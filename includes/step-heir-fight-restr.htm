Boss: Heir (Fight)

Fight the Heir with ice-combo

  1. Please make sure Orange Peril Ring is equipped. Warp to the Heir's arena and initiate her cutscene (not allowed to be skipped in Restricted).
  2. As soon as you are hit with her sword, enter the speedrunner code.
  3. Run up to the Heir and execute the ice combo.
  4. Time is on last hit in Phase 2. If you die in this fight, sadly the run is over as there is no way to re-enter the Heir fight quickly. Good luck!
