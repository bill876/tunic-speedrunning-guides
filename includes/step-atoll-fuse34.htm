NE and SE Fuses

Pray for good crabs

NE Fuse (3)

  • Walk down the right staircase and run against the farthest right point.
  • Target the group of crabs and fire a wand shot.
  • As soon as it strikes, re-target another crab and ice-grapple it.
  • Immediately and quickly run up and activate the fuse.
The crabs are unfortunately a bit RNG; consider freezing one to jam them up.
  • As soon as the fuse lights, orb down to the hook to the south and walk south along the brick catwalk.

SE Fuse (4)

  • Target and ice-grapple the south-most small crab and head SW.
  • Orb across the water to the lower hook.
  • Run past the crabs and around to the SW point, up a couple ladders, and activate the last fuse.
The larger crab can be a good way to damage down to critical HP.
  • Pause, quit, and continue as soon as the power line lights up.
