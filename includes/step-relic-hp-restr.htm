HP Relic (Forest Fortress)

Obtain the third relic after changing the checkpoint.

  1. Execute the wake-up dash sequence again. This time, stop and warp on the first pad.
  2. Navigate to the Forest Fortress pad (top-right) and warp. Immediately save.
  3. Dash south out of the Siege Engine arena and through the Fortress interior. Head east, dash to the Hero's Grave, warp, and dash to the HP Relic. As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, Quit to Menu.
  4. Throw a bomb and death warp.
