Quarry Skip

Clip off the ledge and run directly to Ziggurat

  • Make sure the speedrunner code is active (not as applicable on NG+)
  • Equipment required: Orb, Dagger, Wand
  • Walk to the edge of the broken fuse north of the shrine.
  • Target the nearest Scavenger and grapple him to you.
  • Let them fall a several frames and then ice grapple them.
  • Run to the Ziggurat in the tightest line around the debris possible.
  • Immediately pause, quit, and continue upon entering the Ziggurat (N/A for NG+)
As you are falling after ice-grappling, be sure to use this time to equip your next set of items.
