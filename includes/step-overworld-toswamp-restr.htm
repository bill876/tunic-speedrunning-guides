To Swamp

Run to the Swamp and past the save to the closed gate.

  1. Let the opening crawl of the Dark World play; nothing you can do until it you stand.
  2. Run to the Swamp (up, first right, through the grass, past the SE door, down, and across the light bridge).
  3. Continue into the Swamp. Follow the fuse line and past the save shrine.
