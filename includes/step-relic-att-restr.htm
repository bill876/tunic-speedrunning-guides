ATT Relic (East Forest)

Obtain the fourth relic

  1. Quick warp to the East Forest warp pad (far right). Head to the path beside the Guardhouse toward the Hero's Grave.
  2. After a dash or two into the scene, throw a lure and dash to the bush right of the gate.
  3. Position yourself against the bush next to the gate. Dash up against the bush until you clip into the gate and through. Note: the stick position for dashing through the gate is very specific. It's about 80% up and 10% left of the analog stick range.
  4. Dash to the Hero's Grave, play, and dash quickly to obtain the ATT Relic. As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, quit and continue.
  5. Throw a bomb and death warp.
