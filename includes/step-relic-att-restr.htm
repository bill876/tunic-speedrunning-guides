ATT Relic (East Forest)

Obtain the fourth relic.

  • Quick warp to the East Forest warp pad.
  • Head to the path beside the Guardhouse toward the Hero's Grave.
  • After a dash or two into the scene, throw a lure and dash to the gate.
  • Position yourself against the bush next to the gate. Dash up against the bush until you clip into the gate and through.
Note: the stick position for dashing through the gate is very specific. It's about 80% up and 10% left of the analog stick range.
  • Dash to the Hero's Grave, play, and dash quickly to obtain the ATT Relic.
  • As soon as the light trails meet at the fox, Quit to Menu.
  • Throw a bomb and death warp back to Overworld.
