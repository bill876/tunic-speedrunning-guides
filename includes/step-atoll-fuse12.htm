NW and SW Fuses

Grapple some frogs and birds

NW Fuse (1)

  • Walk down the left staircase and 180 behind it to the beach.
  • Walk to the inner corner and target/wand the frog.
This timing is very tight; if you are slow here, the frog may walk away.
  • When the frog approaches, ice-grapple him and run to activate the fuse.
  • The frog will thaw, approach and may attack but typically will not hit you. You can ice-beam him while praying if you wish to be safe.
  • As soon as the fuse is active, move to the inside pocket of the brick wall and target/orb down to the lower hook.
  • Immediately re-target the hook to the lower-right and grapple to it.

SW Fuse (2)

  • Run south toward the next fuse, hugging the lower side of the area to avoid aggro-ing enemies.
  • When you reach the mountain, walk behind it to the NE edge and ice-grapple the small blue bird at the top.
  • Move away from the frozen bird and activate this fuse.
  • Pause, quit, and continue as soon as the power line lights up.
