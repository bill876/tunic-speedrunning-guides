Upper Ziggurat

Clear the fairies without losing time

  • Run past the fairies and hug the north edge of the upper descending path (to avoid the 3 addl. fairies).
  • At the catwalk corner, turn and dispatch the fairies with the wand.
  • Ice-beam the switch across the gap and menu to sword
  • Your goal now is to ice-grapple it approx. 3-4x, let it thraw and fall, and then ice-grapple it another 3x
  • At this point, you should fall onto the lower part of the tower
