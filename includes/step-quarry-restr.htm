Quarry Run

Avoid Scavengers and enter the Ziggurat

Damage Down

  • Aggro the nearest Scavenger with a wand bolt.
  • Quickly switch to Muffling Bell.
  • Hold target to keep the sniper in view and take a sniper hit.
  • Immediately pause, quit, and continue.

Quarry Run

  • Avoid the scavengers following a specific walking path (see video).
  • If you are running All Relics or want safety mana berries, veer left of the shortcut and open the chest for Mana Berries (3).
  • Run into and down the shortcut.
  • If you are running All Relics, bomb the hidden wall and obtain Lure (2). Otherwise proceed.
  • Make it to the shortcut and activate. Menu to Sword, Dagger, Wand.
  • Descend the two ladders and fall to the ground.
  • Once you hit the ground, quickly swap Muffling Bell with Orange Peril Ring.
  • Run counter-clockwise through the remainder of Quarry and into the Ziggurat.
  • Immediately pause, quit, and continue to reset your maximum health.

Ziggurat Entrance

  • Immediately pause, quit, and continue to reset your maximum health.
  • Run through the rooms and descend on the elevator. Breathe, stretch.
Safety: You can instead menu to Orb instead of Sword at the shortchut and grapple the sniper to the south to avoid having to dodge their shot. This loses a couple seconds but is safer.
