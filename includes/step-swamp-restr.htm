Swamp (Restricted)

Use Muffling Bell to sneak around in the Swamp.

  1. As the door opens to the back-half of the Swamp, equip Muffling Bell.
  2. There are three (3) ghost foxes you must not aggro. Watch the video to learn the specific route you must walk.
  3. Power the fuses and enter the Cathedral. Use the time the door is opening to equip your next items (Orb, Lure, and Wand).

Shoutouts to Glacia for their original tutorial (which shows the location of the ghost foxes).
