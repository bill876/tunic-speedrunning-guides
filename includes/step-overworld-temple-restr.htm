Place Hex Keys

Warp to the Overworld, open the Sealed Temple, and drop keys.

  1. Warp to the Overworld West. Use the wand to clear the bushes.
  2. Activate the Golden Door. While it is opening, equip Glass Cannon.
  3. Run to the first key slot, place the key, and immediately quit and continue. Do this again for the second key slot.
  4. For the third key, you have two options:
    1. the fastest way: toss a bomb onto the steps, walk up, activate the key cutscene, and let the bomb explode, initiating a death warp.
    2. the safest way: place the key, quit and continue, and then throw the bomb. It loses a couple seconds, but it's consistent and safe.
