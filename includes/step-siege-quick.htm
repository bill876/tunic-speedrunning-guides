Boss: Siege (Stunlock)

Use Orange Peril Ring to break poise and stunlock Siege Engine

  • Make sure you are in peril state
  • Equipment required: Sword
  • Run to the Siege Engine and approach from the side.
  • Roll to the middle just behind the center piece behind the face.
  • On first hit, the Siege will start to wake. For this phase
    • If you are at ATT 1, use 1-2 combos and hit 8 times to break poise
    • If you are at ATT 5+, use 1-2 combos and hit 5 times to break poise
  • Once the Siege Engine breaks poise and staggers backwards, hold target and:
    • If you are at ATT 1: use two 1-2-3 combos to break poise. You can basically spam attack until the end of the fight.
    • If you are at ATT 5+: use 1-2 combos and hit 5 times to break poise. Continue this technique until the end of the fight.
  • Once the screen begins to fade from white, use this time to menu for the next step.
  • Obtain the Red Key Screw and run to the plinth.
  • Interact with it, select OK, and immediately pause/quit/continue.
