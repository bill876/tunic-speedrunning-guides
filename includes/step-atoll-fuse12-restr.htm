NE and SE Fuses

Pray for good crabs

  1. Walk up immediately on loading. As you reach the top of the ladder, menu to equip the orb.
  2. Grapple up and out of Frog's Domain. Grapple across to the brick walkway. Exit down to the ramp, run up to first fuse, and activate.
  3. Run down the ramps and toward the middle of the area. Continue south and grapple across the middle. This is a good opportunity to damage down with the giant crab, if you are not at critical HP.
  4. Run to the SE corner of Atoll, climb to and activate the second fuse. Climb down the ladder and head north to the visible chest. Open the chest for 100 bits.
  5. Let the activated fairies kill you after you've collected the bits. Timing is tight if at critical HP.
