NE and SE Fuses

Pray for good crabs

  • Walk up immediately on loading.
  • As you reach the top of the ladder, menu to equip Orb.
  • Grapple up and out of the Frog's Domain entry.
  • Grapple across to the brick catwalk, clip down to the ramp, run up to first fuse, and activate.
  • Run down the ramps and to the middle of the Atoll. Grapple across the middle. This is a good opportunity to damage down with the giant crab, if you are not at low HP.
  • Run to the SE corner of Atoll, climb to and activate the second fuse.
  • Open the chest for 100 bits.
  • Let the activated fairies kill you after you've collected the bits.
