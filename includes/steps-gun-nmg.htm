Use Bomb

Sequence break into the western Overworld

  1. Run up two flights of stairs and head west. Equip the firecracker and throw it at the bushes. Roll through the explosion and head west.
  2. Run down to the Ruined Shop and across to the Envoy.

Roll Past Envoy

More questions than answers. More resets than finishes.

This trick requires 60Hz or less. It is also inconsistent.
  1. Approach the Envoy from the left edge in full run. Immediately after his attack wind-up, roll to the opposite side and attempt to clip past him.
  2. Climb the ladder and enter the Forbidden Pass. Run past the fuse and into the Quarry. Run past the second fuse and to the save point.

Run Through Quarry

It's scary but they usually don't hurt you

  1. Run past the Scavengers and avoid the sniper shot by curving slightly.
  2. Run into the hidden shortcut (right of the boxes) and down into the lower Quarry. Clip all of the slope corners you can. U-turn at the large purple crystal and into the shooting range.
