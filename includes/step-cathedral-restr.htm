Cathedral (Restricted)

Activate the elevator to the Gauntlet.

  1. Run right through the next two rooms and north to the big pink glowy room.
  2. Walk up toward the pool and grapple the ghost fox on the ledge. See the video for precise positioning. You also need to flick your stick while grappling to make sure you are targeting the fox on the ledge.
  3. Continue through the rooms as normal. Hug the inner wall in the room with the birds. Upon entering the room with the two guard foxes, throw a lure.
  4. Continue to the fuse, power it, and quit and continue. Run directly to the Gauntlet elevator and descend. Descend the ladder to the Gauntlet.
