Upgrade ATT

Trigger the sniper and upgrade

  • Roll north and hit the nearest scavenger with a wand bolt.
  • Immediately roll back toward the save and equip Glass Cannon.
  • Quickly initiate an upgrade to ATT 4.
  • The sniper should shoot you and skip the majority of the upgrade cutscene.
  • If the sniper misses, another scavenger should be along shortly with a sword.
