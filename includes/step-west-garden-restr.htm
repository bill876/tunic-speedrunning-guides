West Garden Run

Obtain the Sword and run to the 2nd save

  1. Run past the Hero's Grave and to the filigree door. Enter the code (DRULUR) and obtain the Sword. Equip it as you roll away to save some time.
  2. Run past the first checkpoint, past Terry, and through the water. Continue up and down the stairs, south under the raised walkway, and west past the Chompignoms. 180 up the stone wall and east, past more enemies. Up the stone ramp, east, and south to the second checkpoint. Save here.
