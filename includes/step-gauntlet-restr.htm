Gauntlet (Restricted)

Conquer the Gauntlet and receive the Hero's Laurels.

The Gauntlet is largely the same flow regardless the category or ruleset, as your attack is artificially capped at 1 inside. Besides the first wave, your own personal preference for how to tackle the Gauntlet is largely fine.

Fairy Wave

  1. Immediately activate the fairy wave (far left). Your objective in this wave will be to damage down to peril state to activate Orange Peril Ring for the remaining waves.
  2. Stand on the upper-right of the arena and start sniping the fairies as they spawn. You should be able to take out 10 before any become a threat.
  3. You'll quickly run out of mana--run through the dead fairies to replenish. Rinse and repeat until you are in peril state, then down the last fairy.

Remaining Waves

  • Again, preference: My personal order: Fairies, Mages, Rudelings, Garden Knights, Frogs, Skeletons. I like using the extra lure for the Mages wave.
  • You do not need to activate the health wave to complete the Gauntlet.

Laurels

  1. While the Laurels chest is spawning in, equip bombs. As soon as you acquire Laurels, exit the Gauntlet to the south.
